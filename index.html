Visiting Research Fellow, University of Derby (from April 2026)

I am a business and management scholar whose research sits at the intersections of entrepreneurship, conflict economics, AI in higher education, and decolonisation. I hold a PhD from Queen Mary University of London and am a Fellow of the Higher Education Academy. My work has been published in leading management journals and featured by BERA, Springer, and international media.

Research Interests

My work spans three interconnected streams: the economics of conflict and entrepreneurship in developing economies; the role of AI and technology in transforming higher education; and questions of knowledge, equity, and decolonisation in academic institutions.

Entrepreneurship & Conflict Terrorism & Economic Development AI in Higher Education AI Literacy Inequality & Mobility Decolonisation Digital Entrepreneurship Social Influence & Parasocial Relationships Philanthropy & Social Change Gendered Networks in Academia Business in Emerging Markets Peace Dividends & Growth
